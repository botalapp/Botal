# Botal App Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 26, 2023

Thank you for your trust and choice in Botal. We highly respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy explains how we handle your personal information.

## Data Collected

- **Usage Data:** We collect data about how you use Botal, such as the time you open the app, frequency and cycle of use.

- **Device Information:** As necessary, we may record the information of the device you are using to provide an optimized user experience.

- **Personal Information:** Such as username, email address.

## User-Generated Content

Please note that even though you can create and edit mind models and notes on Botal, we will never access or touch these contents. These content are stored on your device and can only be accessed and controlled by you.

## Use of Data

The data we collect is primarily used to improve our services, to provide you with a better user experience. We may optimize our app based on the device information you use.

## Share and Disclosure

We promise that we will never share your personal data with any third party without your explicit consent. The only exception is when the law requires us to provide information to the government or public institutions.

## Security

We highly value the safety of the information. We take all reasonably possible measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

## Access, Change and Control Your Personal Information

You have the right to access all your personal data in Botal and can modify or delete at any time. If you need further help, such as data export, feel free to contact us.

## Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may periodically update this privacy policy and will notify you through the Botal App before any significant changes occur.

## How to Contact Us

If you have any questions or need help, you can contact us at any time through the following ways:

- Email: botalapp2023@gmail.com

Thank you for using the Botal App! We promise to do our utmost to protect your personal information and privacy.
